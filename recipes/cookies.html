Triple Chocolate Chunk Cookies

Description

Cookies with three different types of chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup milk chocolate
  • 1 bar dark chocolate
  • 3 tbsp white chocolate

Steps

  1. Combine sugar, butter, salt in large bowl. Beat until screamy. Add egg and vanilla, beat until smooth.
  2. In separate bowl, mix flour and baking soda. Slowly add to wet ingredients until mostly incorporated. Fold in chocolate. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 min.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degres F.
  4. Divide dough into 2.25 ounce portions and place onto baking sheet.
  5. Bake until edges are golden brown. Remove from oven and allow to cool and set, about 30 minutes.
