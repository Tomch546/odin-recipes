French Toast

Description

Standard French Toast recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 12 slices of bread

Steps

  1. Measure flour into mxing bowl. Whisk in milk, salt, eggs cinnamon, vanilla and sugar until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium heat.
  3. Soak both sides of bread in mixture until saturated.
  4. Fry each side until golden brown.
