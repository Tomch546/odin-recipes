Pineapple Lemonade

Description

Smoothie made using fresh fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 pineapple
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 2 cups ice cubes
  • Sweetener to taste

Steps

  1. Core and cut pineapple into small chunks. Measure out 2 cups and save juices.
  2. Combine pineapple, half the water, and lemon juice in a blender and blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into pitcher and add remaining water and sweetener to taste. Serve over ice.
